About this book

Quantum physics is taught in fragments. You learn wave-particle duality here, quantum entanglement there, the uncertainty principle somewhere else — but nobody shows you how they all fit together. Quantum Theories: The Missing Big Picture provides exactly that: a unified framework that reveals the complete architecture of quantum theory. This book shows how quantum mechanics, quantum field theory, and quantum evolution form a coherent whole — and gives you the map you need to navigate the entire field with confidence.

What is inside

The complete quantum picture

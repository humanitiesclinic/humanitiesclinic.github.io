About this book

The HSK examination underwent a major restructuring with HSK 3.0 — a more demanding and comprehensive framework than its predecessor. This study guide provides everything you need: a clear explanation of the new HSK 3.0 structure, systematic vocabulary and grammar coverage for each level, and full mock papers for exam practice. Written from a unified framework perspective, the guide does not just drill individual items — it shows you the complete architecture of the examination so you always understand where you are and what you need.
